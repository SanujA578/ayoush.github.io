How to Apply for AYUSH Startup Program

Follow these steps to complete your application for the AYUSH Startup Registration.

Step 1: Create an Account

Register on the AYUSH Startup Portal by creating an account using your email ID. Provide basic details about yourself and your business to begin.

Register Now

Step 2: Submit Business Proposal

Fill in your business details including startup category, product/services, and AYUSH compliance details. Upload required documents like your business plan, licenses, and certifications.

Step 3: Review Process

Once you have submitted your application, it will be reviewed by the AYUSH authorities. If additional information is required, you will be notified via email.

Step 4: Approval and Next Steps

After your application is approved, you will receive a confirmation email with details of your registration and access to further resources to support your startup journey.
